Welcome!


This is where I've decided to share all things knitting.


I've been knitting in total for over ten years, but really fell in love with it back in 2017. I really loved the way hand knit garments looked and decided to dive in head first. I have knit so many things that seemed intimidating at first but once I went for it I realized that it wasn't as bad as I made it out to be!


This is a sweater I had the pleasure of testing the pattern for a very talented sweater designer. Her name is Morgan and she is the face behind Morthunder over on Instagram.


Here we've got a dress that I knit for one of my kiddos. This dress is the Anya dress by Suzie Sparkles over on Ravelry.


This black cardigan is the Kimi Kimono that was designed by Morgane Mathieu of Trust the Mojo Knits.



This beanie is the full moon beanie by Prem Knits, I knit this for a friend and I loved this color choice so much.



These socks are socks that I knit for my son before he was born, they were the first pair of socks I ever knit.


Some of my favorite yarns and tools:


My favorite knitting needles have got to be the ChiaoGoo needles. I have an interchangeable set as well as several other pairs in smaller sizes that didn't come with my interchangeable set.


Some of my all time favorite yarn brands are by Madeline Tosh and Malabrigo. I recently tried Moonglow Yarn Co and I have to say that any time I order yarn from online I get nervous that it won't be as soft as it looks. Well Moonglow Yarn Co.'s yarn is SO soft. I can hardly wait to finish the infinity cowl I'm currently working on from Pacific Knit Co.


Some of my other favorite knitting tools are my iPad and Apple Pencil. These are completely not necessary, but I do love that I can mark off where I'm at in a chart without writing on a printed version of my pattern.
